✏️ ADDRESS FORMATTING (CRITICAL)

- Convert ALL addresses into CAPITALIZED WORD FORMAT

Example:
Subudhipur, Kalinga Vihar → SUBUDHIPUR, KALINGA VIHAR

- Apply capitalization:
  ✔ First letter of each word OR full uppercase (as per template consistency)
  ✔ Maintain commas and spacing exactly
  ✔ No extra spaces before/after commas

- Structure address into clean readable lines if required

- Do NOT overflow or wrap badly

📌 SPECIAL RULE (AGREEMENT DOC)

- LAST SECTION (First Party / Second Party block):

2. Layout must remain SIDE-BY-SIDE (left/right aligned)
3. No shifting or breaking into next line/page

---

📅 DATE FORMATTING (VERY IMPORTANT)

- Only underline NUMBERS:

Example:
❌ 12 (Day) 04 (Month) 2026 (Year)  → fully underlined ❌

- Words like (Day), (Month), (Year) must NOT be underlined
